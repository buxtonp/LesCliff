Les Cliff

Artist and modeller

Les Cliff is a talented artist and modeller whose work is characterised by an extraordinary level of detail and realism. 
Les has appeared on the Great Model Railway Challenge 2019, where his backgrounds and models gained great respect from the judges and other competitors. His construction skills were also much utilised when he took part in Dick Strawbridge's Biggest Little Railway in the World, where he helped construct a model railway that ran across Scotland from Inverness to Fort William.
For those who want a unique, highly detailed model, background or diorama, he undertakes a limited number of commissions a year and he would be delighted to discuss your individual requirements with you.
