Les Cliff - Dioramic Artist

Bespoke, detailed, realistic 

Les Cliff is an experienced artist and model maker with a keen eye for detail, and has appeared on television with his modelling. He creates extraordinarily detailed dioramas and backgrounds which would enhance your model railway or wargaming scenarios, and each commission is bespoke. Whether you want to recreate the heyday of steam, a landscape for charging cavalry, urban battlefield or the dystopian fantasy of 40K, Les can produce a realistic and unique work that is tailored specifically to your requirements and turn your concept into a tangible reality  .
